Volunteer Program

Our volunteers have a desire to share knowledge, and to mentor youth & young adults. They teach basic and advanced programming skills, robotics, and more. We're are a family of developers and designers who have the desire, patience, and ability to work with students in website development and design, app development, UX/UI research and design, graphic design, robotics, and game development, using cutting edge computer science concepts.
